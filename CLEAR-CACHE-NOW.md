Your browser has cached old JavaScript files. The Onboarding page has been updated to look IDENTICAL to the Exit Management page, but your browser is showing you old files.

## THE SOLUTION - DO EXACTLY THIS:

### Option 1: Hard Reload with Developer Tools (RECOMMENDED)

1. **Go to:** `http://localhost:5173` in your current browser

2. **Press F12** (this opens Developer Tools)

3. **Right-click the refresh button** (the circular arrow button at the top of the browser, NOT the F5 key)

4. **Select:** "Empty Cache and Hard Reload" or "Hard Reload"

5. **Close Developer Tools** (Press F12 again)

6. **Login** and go to Onboarding & Probation

---

### Option 2: Clear All Browser Data

#### Chrome:
1. Press `Cmd + Shift + Delete` (Mac) or `Ctrl + Shift + Delete` (Windows)
2. Select "Cached images and files"
3. Click "Clear data"
4. Close browser completely
5. Reopen and go to `http://localhost:5173`

#### Firefox:
1. Press `Cmd + Shift + Delete` (Mac) or `Ctrl + Shift + Delete` (Windows)
2. Select "Cache"
3. Click "Clear"
4. Close browser completely
5. Reopen and go to `http://localhost:5173`

#### Safari:
1. Go to Develop → Empty Caches
2. Close browser completely
3. Reopen and go to `http://localhost:5173`
