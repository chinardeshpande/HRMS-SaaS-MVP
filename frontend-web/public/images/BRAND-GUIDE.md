## 📐 Logo Usage Guidelines

### Minimum Sizes
| Asset | Minimum | Recommended |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| Full Logo | 140px width | 220px width |
| Icon Only | 32px | 64px |

### Clear Space
- Maintain padding equal to the height of "HR" text
- Don't crowd with other elements
- Allow logo to breathe

### ✅ Do
- Use on white, light, or dark backgrounds
- Maintain aspect ratio when scaling
- Use provided SVG files (vector format)
- Keep gradients intact
- Position prominently when space allows

### ❌ Don't
- Change colors or remove gradients
- Rotate, skew, or distort
- Add effects (shadows, outlines)
- Place on busy/patterned backgrounds
- Separate elements arbitrarily
- Recreate or redraw the logo

## 🌟 Design Philosophy

### The Aurora Metaphor

**Aurora** = Dawn breaking, light emerging through darkness

This logo captures:
- 🌅 **Hope** — New beginnings, fresh starts
- 🎨 **Beauty** — Vibrant, flowing, artistic
- 📈 **Growth** — Rising trajectory, transformation
- 🤝 **Humanity** — People (dots) within the design
- ✨ **Innovation** — Forward-thinking, modern
- 💎 **Premium** — Sophisticated, not generic

### Why These Colors?

The aurora borealis displays vibrant ribbons of color in the night sky — a natural phenomenon representing beauty, wonder, and transformation. Our palette captures:

- **Blues/Teals:** Trust, professionalism, depth
- **Purple:** Creativity, wisdom, dawn breaking
- **Pink:** Warmth, humanity, care
- **Gold/Orange:** Optimism, energy, sunrise

### HRMS Connection

The flowing ribbons represent:
- **Employee journeys** from onboarding to growth to exit
- **Continuous transformation** and development
- **Connected platform** where all paths flow together
- **Light and clarity** in people management
