# AuroraHR Brand Guide

## Logo Overview

The AuroraHR logo represents **people, connection, and growth** through a sunrise metaphor. Three people connected through flowing arcs symbolize collaboration, community, and the dawn of new possibilities in HR management.

## Logo Design Elements

### 1. Three People Circles (Top)
- **Symbolism**: Collaboration, team, community
- **Colors**: Primary blue (#0A66C2)
- **Sizes**: Center person (30px), sides (24px)
- **Meaning**: People are at the heart of HR

### 2. Connecting Threads (Dashed Lines)
- **Symbolism**: Connection, communication, relationships
- **Style**: Dashed lines with opacity 0.14
- **Meaning**: People connected through platform

### 3. Main Arc (Sunrise)
- **Symbolism**: Dawn, new beginnings, growth
- **Style**: Thick arc (40px stroke)
- **Meaning**: The aurora (dawn) of better HR management

### 4. Inner Echo Arc
- **Symbolism**: Resonance, impact, expansion
- **Style**: Thinner arc (18px stroke) with opacity 0.22
- **Meaning**: Ripple effect of good HR practices

### 5. Horizon Baseline
- **Symbolism**: Foundation, stability, platform
- **Style**: Thin line with opacity 0.13
- **Meaning**: Solid foundation for growth

### 6. Bottom Anchor Dots
- **Symbolism**: Grounding, connection points
- **Center**: Solid with white core (sun rising)
- **Sides**: Semi-transparent anchors
- **Meaning**: Platform touchpoints for people

## Logo Usage Guidelines

### ✅ Do

- Use the SVG files provided (vector, scalable)
- Maintain clear space around logo (equal to height of icon)
- Use on white or very light backgrounds primarily
- Scale proportionally
- Use navbar version for headers (48-60px height)
- Use icon version for favicons (16×16, 32×32, 64×64)
- Keep logo readable at all sizes

### ❌ Don't

- Change the color (#0A66C2 only)
- Rotate, skew, or distort the logo
- Add effects (shadows, outlines, glows)
- Separate icon from text arbitrarily
- Use on busy or patterned backgrounds
- Make logo smaller than 120px width (full version)
- Recreate or redraw the logo

## Design Philosophy

### The Sunrise Metaphor
The logo captures the concept of **dawn** - new beginnings, hope, and the start of something better. Just as the sun rises to illuminate the world, AuroraHR illuminates HR processes and empowers people.

### People First
Three connected people emphasize that HR is fundamentally about **human relationships, collaboration, and community**. The platform connects people and enables them to thrive.

### Connection & Flow
The flowing arcs and dashed connection lines represent the **seamless flow of information, communication, and support** through the AuroraHR platform.

### Professional Blue
LinkedIn blue (#0A66C2) conveys:
- **Trust**: Enterprise-grade reliability
- **Professional**: Serious about HR
- **Modern**: Tech-forward platform
- **Connected**: Part of professional ecosystem

## File Specifications

### Formats
All logos provided as **SVG** (Scalable Vector Graphics):
- ✅ Infinite scaling without quality loss
- ✅ Small file size
- ✅ Works everywhere (web, print, mobile)
- ✅ Editable in design tools

### Export for Raster
If PNG or JPG needed:
- Export from SVG at **2×-3× final display size**
- Use transparent background (PNG)
- Optimize for web (TinyPNG, ImageOptim)
